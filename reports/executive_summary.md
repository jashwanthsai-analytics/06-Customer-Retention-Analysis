# Executive Summary

The analysis identified a churn rate of 26.54%.

Key churn drivers include:

- Month-to-Month contracts
- Fiber Optic internet service
- High monthly charges
- Short customer tenure
- Electronic Check payment method

Business recommendations focus on improving customer retention through contract migration, pricing optimization, retention campaigns, and enhanced customer support.

Expected Outcome:

- Reduce churn rate by 3-5%
- Increase retention rate
- Improve customer lifetime value
- Reduce revenue loss from churn
