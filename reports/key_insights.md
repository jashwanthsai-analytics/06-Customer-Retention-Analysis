# Key Insights

## Executive Overview

- Total Customers: 7,043
- Active Customers: 5,174
- Churned Customers: 1,869
- Churn Rate: 26.54%

## Contract Analysis

- Month-to-Month customers have the highest churn rate.
- One-Year and Two-Year contracts show significantly lower churn.

## Internet Service Analysis

- Fiber Optic customers churn more frequently than DSL users.

## Tenure Analysis

- Customers in the first year show the highest churn.
- Churn decreases as customer tenure increases.

## Payment Method Analysis

- Electronic Check customers experience the highest churn rate.

## Demographics Analysis

- Senior Citizens churn at a higher rate.
- Gender has minimal impact on churn behavior.
