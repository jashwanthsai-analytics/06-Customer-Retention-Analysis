# Recommendations

## Contract Strategy

Encourage customers to migrate from Month-to-Month plans to annual contracts.

## Customer Retention

Launch targeted retention campaigns for customers in their first year.

## Pricing Strategy

Review Fiber Optic pricing and service quality.

## Payment Method Optimization

Promote Auto-Pay and Credit Card payment methods through incentives.

## Senior Citizen Programs

Provide customized plans and support for senior customers.

## Customer Experience

Address service-related complaints identified in churn reasons analysis.
